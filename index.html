From an early age, I’ve had a passion for technology, business and anything finance related. It therefore came as no surprise when I studied MEng Computer Engineering at the University of Portsmouth whilst simultaneously starting my own business.

A lot of people were therefore confused why I didn’t continue my own business upon graduating and instead joined NATS (UK air traffic control) on their engineering graduate scheme. The honest answer is that I love the criticality of air traffic control - something that most people take for granted and don’t even think about.

So what about my passion for business and finance? That’s where The Wheal Group fits in. Before you ask, no, I don’t want to one day quit my day job and run it full-time. Consider it more like a hobby - a hobby that I pay other people to run.

John Wheal - Founder of The Wheal Group
